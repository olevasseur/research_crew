Generate product ideas only for normalized problems that have clear supporting evidence.

For each idea, output:

- idea_name
- based_on_pain_point_id
- problem_solved
- target_user
- product_concept
- business_model
- why_users_might_pay
- implementation_notes
- source_urls

Rules:
- **product_concept**: Write a clear 1–3 sentence description of what the product is and what it does, so a reader understands the idea from the name and concept alone (e.g. “A browser extension that…” or “A SaaS dashboard that…”).
- **source_urls**: Use the precise article/page URLs from the raw evidence for this problem (full URLs to specific posts or articles), not index or category URLs.
