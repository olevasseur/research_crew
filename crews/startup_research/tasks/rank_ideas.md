- rank
- idea_name
- **what_it_does** — A clear 2–4 sentence description of what the product is, who it’s for, and what it does. A reader should understand the idea without prior context (e.g. “WorkflowBuddy is a browser extension that…” not just the name).
- based_on_pain_point_id
- evidence_strength
- mention_count
- recurring_revenue_potential
- founder_fit
- leverage
- complexity
- why_this_could_work
- main_risk
- source_urls

Rules:
- **source_urls**: Include the precise article/page URLs that support this idea (full URLs to specific posts or articles from the evidence), not index or category URLs.
